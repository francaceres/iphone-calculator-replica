...the replica

I began working on this replica as a way of practicing my recently acquired front end development skills. The technologies used here are HTML, CSS (including some SCSS) and vanilla JavaScript as they're the ones I've learned so far on my courses.
What surprised me the most about the challenge was to find out that coding a calculator isn't as simple as I thought it would be. I'm proud to have come up with a solution that is my own though, as I tried to figure it out by myself without looking for examples on the internet. Please do let me know if you find a better way to do it!
Finally, I'd like to clarify that there are still a few details that need to be solved on the calculator, like the functionality of the +/- and % buttons, and the overflow of the numbers of the display panels, which I will try to solve in time.
Anyway, thanks for checking out my project!
